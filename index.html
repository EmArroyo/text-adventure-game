Escape the mansion

Press any key to start

The objective of the game is to escape the mansion. To do so, the player can navigate the different rooms and floors in the mansion, while picking up objects and talking to different characters in their journey. The only door to exit the mansion is guarded by the gatekeeper, who will request an item from the player in order to open the door. If the player hands over the wrong item, the game will end.


Controls:

The player can move between rooms by typing the directions he/she wishes to go. The options are:

  • Up
  • Down
  • North
  • South
  • East
  • West
Other actions available to the player are:
  • Talk. Use it in a room where there is another character to listen to what they have to say.
  • Pick-up. Use it to collect items in the different rooms.
  • Give. Use it to give an item to the gatekeeper. Your will be asked to confirm which item you want to hand over.
